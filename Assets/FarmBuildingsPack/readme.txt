This package contains art assets you need to make your beautiful farm in your game!  
This package can be for farming games, barn buddy games, match3 games, bubble shooter games or other games. 
All of the assets are in HD Quality and contains the PSD so you can create new assets based on them!

It includes more than 40 items:
- 12 houses include cartoon houses，freight houses，barns etc;
- 24 trees and grass include apple trees, pine trees etc；
- 2 haystacks;
- Bridge;
- Lake;
- Cloud;

It also includes 10 backgrounds: 
- 3 Spring farm backgrounds;
- 2 Summer farm backgrounds;
- 3 Autumn farm backgrounds;
- 2 Winter farm backgrounds;
